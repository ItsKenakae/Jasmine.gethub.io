Step 1: Break The Ice

— The first step of all friendships is actually interacting with each other! Approach them and start a conversation. Many people accomplish this with an icebreaker, a casual question meant to dispell any awkwardness./p>

Some common icebreakers include:

  • Hi, my name is [inset name], what's yours?
  • How was your day today?
  • The weather is nice right now, isn't it?
